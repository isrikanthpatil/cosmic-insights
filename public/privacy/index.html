Privacy Policy

Astropanth · Effective date: 17 June 2026

Astropanth ("we", "us", the "App") provides personalised astrology and numerology insights based on your birth details. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Astropanth you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address and name when you create an account or sign in with Google.
  • Birth details — your date of birth, time of birth (optional), place of birth, and gender. We use these solely to calculate your astrology chart and numerology numbers.
  • Questions you ask — the messages you send to the AskAstro feature, so we can generate a response.
  • Limited technical data — your approximate IP address, used only to apply rate limits (for example, the free daily question limit for guests). We do not build advertising profiles.

You can browse the astrology and numerology sections as a guest without creating an account. In guest mode, birth details you enter are stored only on your device.

How we use your information

  • To generate your astrology and numerology readings and daily/weekly horoscopes.
  • To power the AskAstro chat assistant.
  • To create and secure your account and send account-related emails (such as verification and password resets).
  • To protect the service from abuse.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Service providers

We use a small number of trusted providers to operate the App:

  • Google — if you choose "Sign in with Google", Google provides us your name and email to create your account.
  • AI processing — the question you type into AskAstro, together with your chart summary, is sent to an AI language model provider to generate a reply. It is used only to produce your answer.
  • Email delivery (Brevo) — used to send account emails such as verification and password reset links.
  • Hosting — our application and database run on our own servers hosted with a cloud infrastructure provider.

Data storage and security

Your account data is stored in our own database on secured servers, transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. Passwords are stored in hashed form and are never visible to us. While no system can be guaranteed perfectly secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your account information for as long as your account exists. You can permanently delete your account and its associated data at any time from Profile → Settings → Delete Account in the App. You may also email us to request deletion.

Children's privacy

Astropanth is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, use the in-app controls or contact us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and, where appropriate, notifying you in the App.

Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at support@astropanth.com.

Astropanth provides insights for reflection and entertainment and does not offer medical, financial, or legal advice.
