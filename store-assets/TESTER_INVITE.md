# Astropanth — closed test invite

Google requires **12 testers opted in for 14 continuous days** before the app can go to production. Send the message below to at least 12 people (friends, family, colleagues — anyone with an Android phone and a Google account). Ask them to keep the app installed for ~2 weeks.

**Before sending:** in Play Console → Test and release → Testing → Closed testing → **Testers** tab → "How testers join your test", copy the **opt-in link** and paste it where it says [OPT-IN LINK] below. (It usually looks like `https://play.google.com/apps/testing/com.astropanth.cosmicinsights`.)

Also make sure each person's **Gmail address is in your tester email list** — the link only works for emails you've added.

---

## Message to send (WhatsApp / email)

Hi! I've built an astrology + numerology app called **Astropanth** and I'm doing a private test before launch. Could you help by being a tester for ~2 weeks? It's quick:

1. On your **Android phone**, open this link and tap **"Become a tester"**:
   [OPT-IN LINK]
2. Then tap the link to **download Astropanth on Google Play** and install it.
3. Just open it once or twice and have a look — that's it. Please keep it installed for about 2 weeks.

Important: use the same Google account / email you gave me, or it won't let you in. Thanks so much — it really helps me get it launched! 🙏

---

## If someone says the link doesn't work
- Confirm the exact Gmail they're using is the one you added to the tester list.
- They must tap **"Become a tester"** on the opt-in page first, *then* install from Play.
- It can take a few minutes after opting in for the Play listing to appear for them.

## What counts toward the requirement
- 12+ testers who **opt in** (not just receive the link).
- They stay opted in for **14 days**. The sooner all 12 join, the sooner the clock completes.
- After 14 days, "Apply for production access" unlocks in Play Console.
